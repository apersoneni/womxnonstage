Our Story

Built for
Every Stage

Womxn on Stage connects, celebrates, and supports womxn artists and entrepreneurs worldwide. We create organic networking spaces where creatives can grow their visibility, unlock business opportunities, and receive direct feedback on their ideas and portfolios.

In an age of increasing digital isolation, we foster real-life community, collaboration, and meaningful connections. We use "womxn" to intentionally include cis women, trans women, and non-binary people. All genders are welcome.

Founded by Annabel Personeni — singer-songwriter, entrepreneur, and world traveller with a ukulele — from the stage of Ekstase, Zurich.

✦

Helping Others

We rise by lifting each other. Every stage we build creates the next one.

✦

Open-Mindedness

Diverse voices and perspectives are welcomed and respected — always.

✦

Synergy

Collaboration over competition. We create networking spaces that actually work.

✦

Real Community

In an age of digital isolation, we show up in person — and that changes everything.

The Founder

Annabel
Personeni

Singer-songwriter, entrepreneur, and world traveller with a ukulele. Annabel has performed original music across the globe and built creative communities along the way.

Inspired by her experiences on stage, she founded Womxn on Stage to celebrate artists, painters, and entrepreneurs who dare to bring their vision into the world.

"Collaboration over competition. Synergy over ego."
