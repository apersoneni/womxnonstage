About Us
Short version — for captions & bios
Womxn on Stage is a monthly showcase celebrating womxn artists, entrepreneurs and painters worldwide. We create real-life spaces where creatives grow their visibility and build genuine community. Collaboration over competition. Synergy over ego.
Long version — for press, newsletters & bios
Womxn on Stage connects, celebrates, and supports womxn artists and entrepreneurs worldwide. Founded by Annabel Personeni in Zurich in 2025, we create organic networking spaces where creatives can grow their visibility, unlock business opportunities, and receive direct feedback on their ideas and portfolios. In an age of increasing digital isolation, we foster real-life community, collaboration, and meaningful connections. We use "womxn" to intentionally include cis women, trans women, and non-binary people. All genders are welcome. Collaboration over competition. Synergy over ego.
